Lasagna

Description

This recipe was smuggled into Canada during the Second World War. It was considered so delicious that it was a direct threat to Canada. It had to be locked into a vault under parliament.

  • 1 Box of Lasagna Noodles
  • 1 LBS of Beef
  • 2 Cans of Tomato Sauce
  • 2 Carrots
  • 250G of Mozzarella Cheese
  1. Preheat Oven to 425 degree celcius.
  2. Layer noodles on the bottom of a lasagna pan.
  3. Add beef, tomato sauce, carrots, and mozzarella cheese.
  4. Repeat steps 2 and 3 until you are out of ingredients.
  5. Add additional cheese to the top of the lasagna.
